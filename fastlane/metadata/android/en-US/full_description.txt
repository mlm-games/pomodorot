Pomodorot is a timer app which can be used to set 20 minute breaks where you move away from your computer or also can be used as a pomodoro timer. 

Currently it's screen blocking capabilities is only available in desktops, but plan to add it as a setting for android

If you want an app which pauses or doesn't count down when not open directly on the screen (not in background), then this could work for you. .

Features :

	. Variable time options
	. Minimal
	. Easy on the eyes
	. Open source
	. Fully free with no ads

More info on the itch website: https://ragebreaker.itch.io/pomodorot
